Chicory: A Colorful Tale

August 23rd, 2021/Edit
chicory

I enjoyed playing this game. The themes it explores were deep. The music matched the environments. The boss fights remind me of Undertale.

Chicory: A Colorful Tale is a 2D adventure colouring game. I first heard about it on the Triple Click podcast (Episode 63, The Games of Summer).

End Screen

I cleared the game in 9 hours. Near the end, I used this map tool by JdavisBro which let me know which collectibles I was missing.
